### CPM — Cost per 1,000 impressions

Best for reach, brand and formats where the view itself does the job (Popunder, Interstitial). You control spend; AutoBid controls the rest.

### CPC — Cost per click

Best when your landing page converts and you just need qualified visitors (In-Page Push, Native, Banner).

### CPA — Cost per action

You define the conversion; you pay when it happens. Best for tested funnels with tracking in place.

### Smart bidding layer

Applies to all models: AutoBid to a target eCPA, per-placement custom bids, auto-blacklist rules, and a Traffic Estimator so you can price a geo before committing budget.
