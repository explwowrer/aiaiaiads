## 1. What are cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember your preferences and understand how you interact with it.

## 2. Cookies we use

**Essential cookies:** Required for the website and platform to function. These cannot be disabled. Examples: session authentication, security tokens.

**Analytics cookies:** Help us understand how visitors use our website. We use this data to improve our content and user experience. These cookies collect anonymous, aggregated information.

**Functional cookies:** Remember your preferences such as language selection and display settings.

**Advertising cookies:** Used in our ad-serving technology to deliver relevant advertisements, cap frequency, and measure campaign performance.

## 3. Third-party cookies

Some cookies are set by third-party services we use, such as analytics providers and payment processors. These third parties have their own privacy policies.

## 4. Managing cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling essential cookies may prevent you from using certain features of the Platform.

Most browsers allow you to: view existing cookies; accept or reject cookies; delete cookies; set preferences for specific websites.

## 5. Changes to this policy

We may update this Cookie Policy as our technology or legal requirements change. The current version is always available on this page.
